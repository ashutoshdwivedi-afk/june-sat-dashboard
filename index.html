Nat Habit · Customer Support

Support Pulse — June 2026

Compiled from 1,541 customer feedback entries across Chat and the newly launched Social Media DM channel, plus 123 post-escalation surveys. This is the full picture of where customers stood with us this month, team by team, agent by agent.

Month read: Chat CSAT eased slightly from 85.21% in May to 84.30% in June, mainly on Courier delays (71.15% CSAT). But the new Social Media DM channel opened strong at 98.15% CSAT, lifting the blended direct-channel number to 85.27% — a net improvement month-on-month. Escalation-stage CSAT (70.73%) stays naturally lower, since these are already unhappy because these are failure case or delayed delivery cases mostly.
